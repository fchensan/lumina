Bridges for Enterprise

About CCA#

Bridges for Enterprise (Singapore) is an international student-run organization providing pro-bono advice to social enterprises. Our organization is a part of a larger network of other BfE hubs in New York (New York University) and Cambridge (University of Cambridge)

Why Should You Join?#

There are 4 major divisions in the club with each of them offering different learning opportunities:

  1. Consulting Team: Learn to research, propose and deliver consulting advice to real life social enterprise clients, may include giving advice on marketing strategy, market expansion strategy, etc
  2. Financial Advisory Team: Learn to research, propose and deliver financial advisory advice to real life social enterprise clients, may including building financial models, designing pitch decks, etc
  3. Growth & Partnerships Team: Learn to source, liaise and pitch with professionals within the consulting industry for the organization support network
  4. Startup Recruitment Team: Learn to source, liaise and promote the club to accelerators, communities and groups of social enterprises to take part in our program

Activites/Events#

There are 4 major divisions in the club and each of the roles are explained as follows:

  1. Consulting Team: Provide consulting advice for real life clients in the social enterprise sector, may give advice on marketing strategy, operations strategy, etc
  2. Financial Advisory Team: Provide financial advisory advice for real life clients in the social enterprise sector, may make financial models, pitch decks, etc
  3. Growth & Partnerships Team: Liaise with professionals from the consulting industry to build up BfE mentorship network
  4. Startup Recruitment Team: Liaise with potential social enterprises partners to promote our BfE programme
