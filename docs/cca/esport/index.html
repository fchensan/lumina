NUS Esport Gaming

About CCA#

NUSEG organises gaming competitions in NUS, ranging from Mobile Legends, DotA, LOL, Hearthstone, and Overwatch. We also play the video games mentioned. This CCAis created by gamers for gamers.

Why Should You Join?#

If you like to play games, need friends to push rank together with or even getting recommendation letter out of game in NUS, here is the place

Activites/Events#

Weekly playing specific game that you choose (30 mins session weekly, can be player or spectator)
